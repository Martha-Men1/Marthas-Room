ABOUT

Hey I'm Martha, I attend the University of Waterloo as a Computer Science major. In my off time I like to DJ for my friends and practice playing the guitar. I have interests in activism in tech such as learning to improve facial recognition LLM's to better be accessable for Black and POC individuals.

The lack of diversity in the dataset perpetuates and amplifies societal biases in machine learning models (MLMs), leading to association biases, a phenomenon where models incorrectly link certain features with specific groups. This issue is particularly pronounced in facial recognition models (FRMs), due to inadequate representation of diverse phenotypes results in discriminatory outcomes. A goal of mine is to close this gap and promote equality in tech.
